Tony Stark

(1970-2023)

A timeline of tony stark


  • May 29, 1970: Tony Stark Is Born

  • 1987: Tony Graduates Summa Cum Laude From MIT

  • December 1991: Tony Loses His Parents To An Alleged Car Crash

  • 1992: Tony Takes Over Stark Industries

  • February-March 2009: Tony Is Taken Prisoner After Demonstrating The Jericho Prototype, Eventually Escaping After Creating A Makeshift Battle Suit

  • May 2012: Tony Unites The Avengers To Stop Loki's Chitauri Incursion Of Earth

  • December 2012: Tony Stops Aldrich Killian From Assassinating The President

  • May 2015: Tony Creates Ultron In A Bid To Protect The World From Future Onslaughts, And This Results In The Battle Of Sokovia

  • May 2016: Tony Fights Captain America And His Allies Over Surrendering Bucky Barnes And The Signing Of The Sokovia Accords

  • 2017: Tony Mentors Peter Parker And Gets Engaged To Pepper Potts

  • April 2018: Tony And The Avengers Fail To Stop Thanos From Uniting The Infinity Stones

  • May 2018: Tony Returns To Earth After Captain Marvel Rescues Him

  • 2019: Tony Becomes A Father After Pepper Gives Birth To Morgan Stark

  • October 2023: Tony Solves Time Travel, Helps Orchestrate The Infinity Stone Time Heist And Snaps Thanos Out Of Existence, Perishing From The Strain On His Body
